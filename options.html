Sadly Chrome offers no way of accessing the query URLs of configured search engines so you may have to specify your own.
You can find your personal settings over at chrome://settings/searchEngines.
This extension takes the query URL and replaces the characters %s with your processed query.
Take a look at these example queries from big search engines:

  • https://www.google.com/search?q=%s
  • https://www.bing.com/search?q=%s
  • https://uk.search.yahoo.com/search?p=%s
  • https://www.ecosia.org/search?q=%s
  • https://duckduckgo.com/?q=%s
